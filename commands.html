Commands


-play

[Link or search query]


Plays audio from YouTube or Soundcloud.


Aliases: p


-playlist

[YouTube Playlist URL | Playlist Name]


Play a playlist from youtube.


Aliases: pl


-queue


Show the music queue and now playing.


Aliases: q


-remove

[Queue Number]


Remove song from the queue.


-pause


Pause the currently playing music.


-resume


Resume currently playing music.


-search

[Video Name]


Search and select videos to play.


-shuffle


Shuffle queue.


-skip


Skip the currently playing song.


-skipto

[Queue Number]


Skip to the selected queue number.


Aliases: st


-stop


Stops the music.


-volume

[Use a number between 0 - 100]


Change volume of currently playing music.


Aliases: v


-np


Show now playing song.


-lyrics

[name of a music]


Get lyrics for the currently playing son.


Aliases: ly


-loop


Toggle music loop.


Aliases: l


-help


Display all commands and descriptions.


Aliases: h


-invite


Bot invitation!


Aliases: inviteme


-stats


Bot statistics.


Aliases: botinfo
